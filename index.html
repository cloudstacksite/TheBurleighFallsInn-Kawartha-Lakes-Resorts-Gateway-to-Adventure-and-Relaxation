Burleigh Falls Inn: A Rich History and Stunning Location

For over a century, the Burleigh Falls community has been a welcoming beacon in the Kawartha Lakes area, serving as a gateway between Lovesick Lake and Stoney Lake. Situated on the serene 11-acre Burleigh Island, this historic inn offers breathtaking panoramic views—ranging from the stunning sunrise over Stoney Lake to the spectacular sunsets over the dams of Lovesick Lake.

With a legacy of 160 years, the Burleigh Falls Inn is steeped in character, offering more than just a place to stay. It's a chance to step back in time, feel the history of the place, and create your own stories by the water. If the walls could talk, they would share tales of the generations of guests who have stayed here, making unforgettable memories.

What to Do at Burleigh Falls Inn?

Burleigh Falls Inn offers a wide variety of activities, both on-site and nearby, making it a haven for outdoor lovers, history buffs, and anyone in need of a peaceful escape.

1. Explore The Falls and The Kawarthas

One of the main attractions near the Burleigh falls inn Ontario is the beautiful Burleigh Falls itself. The falls, located on the Trent-Severn Waterway, are a scenic spot perfect for photography, sightseeing, or simply enjoying the rushing waters. You can take in the breathtaking views from the inn’s grounds, or venture out on a boat tour to get up close to the falls.

Beyond the falls, the Kawartha Lakes area is known for its vast array of outdoor activities. Whether you prefer kayaking, boating, hiking, or simply relaxing by the lake, this region offers something for everyone.

Outdoor Adventures in Every Season

  • Spring/Summer: Canoeing, kayaking, and fishing are popular activities on both Stoney Lake and Lovesick Lake. The warm weather also brings opportunities for hiking and exploring nearby parks and trails.
  • Fall: Experience the vibrant autumn foliage with scenic drives or guided hikes. Fall is also an excellent time for fishing, with an abundance of fish species to catch.
  • Winter: The Kawarthas offer plenty of winter activities such as ice fishing, snowshoeing, and cross-country skiing, making Burleigh Falls Inn a wonderful place for a winter getaway.
